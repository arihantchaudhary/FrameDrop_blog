Keshari Chapter 2

Published -- June 27   •   2min read

Audiences are calling Kesari Chapter 2 a strong emotional ride, especially praising Akshay Kumar’s intense performance and the powerful courtroom scenes. Many viewers felt the film did justice to the story of Jallianwala Bagh, while some thought a few scenes were a bit over-dramatic. Still, the majority appreciated the bold storytelling, patriotic feel, and impactful dialogues. It’s a movie that leaves you thinking, and for many, that’s what made it worth the watch.
