GOODBOY

Published -- June 27   •   2min read

Goodboy is one of those shows that feels genuine and heartwarming without trying too hard. The storyline is simple but relatable, and the characters are written in a way that actually makes you care about them. It doesn’t go overboard with drama or fake emotions, which is refreshing. Some parts may feel slow, but the honesty and small moments make it worth the watch. If you're into feel-good stories with a real touch, Goodboy delivers just that.
