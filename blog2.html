F1: The Movie

Published -- June 27   •   3min read

“F1: The Movie” is a visually thrilling ride that captures the speed and excitement of real Formula 1 racing with stunning cinematography and intense race sequences. Brad Pitt and Damson Idris deliver strong performances, and the film feels authentic thanks to real F1 circuits and races. However, the story falls into predictable underdog tropes, with underdeveloped side characters and a weak romantic subplot. While it’s a treat for F1 fans and action lovers, the emotional depth and originality don’t quite match the visual spectacle.
